About

About Refugee Stars

Founded in 2016
We show kindness and generousity to the refugees.

Refugee Stars is a project started by refugees abroad to help refugees in refugee camps. We provide them with food, shelter and other basic needs. We sponsor families of some members to get immigrant visa to USA, Canada and other countries.

Our organisation is membership based where members enjoy many benefits. Every interested one of our stars or members gets a free trip to Africa to witness the works we do in the refugee camps we work with. We also visa sponsor families of our stars or members. You also enjoy being a share holder in chains of businesses that support our charitable works. Taxi businesses, schools, hotels and others.

Our Mission

Change lives of refugees one at a time through membership generousity.

Sponsor refugee families of some of our members to get immigrant visas abroad.

Enhance our membership through business union development.

If you came from kyangwali, please give back!

There are thousands of people who came from kyangwali and other refugee camps in Africa. These people are happily resettled abroad. But most of them, don't really know how the process worked. It all begun with generosity and kindness. People in nations that have gone foward give donations to ogranisation like UNHCR and others. These organisation then deal with the government to help resettle you abroad.

At Refugee Stars we are reproducing the same process. This time its the refugees who were resettled who are putting hands together to that magic can happen.We are so many abroad that with proper management we can reach the capacity to sponsor our own families. People who came from kyangwali are over thousands. At Refugee Stars we have set a goal of reaching atleast 500 people who came from Kyangwali. Each one contributes a minimum of $25 once a month, which is just the price of a few burgers you eat in one day. We have a trusted and secure system already put in place whereby the money will be coming out automatically without hustle. If you do the math 500 kyangwali people giving $25 once a month equals $12,000 a month and $150,000 per year.

If you think about it, imagine your family getting sponsored by only your $25 donation to our membership organisation. Together we can do so much. Monthly you spend over $150 eating unhealthy burgers and pizzas. Just $25 once a month, you could sponsor your family abroad. Therefore join us in this revolution. We are already operating, a TV is already set up. It will be bringing us updates about the families we are helping and also help reach more donors. We have capable and trustworthy individuals managing our organisations. Just by $25 once a month the price of pizza you can help resettle your own family. Join us today!

Event Title

Our membership organisation in a nutshell.

Our organisation started as a project by one person who was doing all the amazing things alone. When others saw the works he was doing, they were so quick to join. We are a membership based organisation were members enjoy alot of things. We also accept generousity from other non members. We have 3 projects that we work on.

  • Generous giving projects
  • Sponsor a family project
  • Business union project.

All these projects are supported by our members. Each member contribute a donations of $25 once a month which is equal to the price of pizza. This money is enough to keep us going. We have a team of individuals capable of managing these funds. They are dedicated and trust worthy. In the generous giving project, we give basic needs to the poor and vulnerable family in the camp. We provide shelter, education, food and other basic needs.

We don't stop on giving, we have two other projects which mainly benefits our members. We do this as a way of saying thank you to our members. The projects are family sponsorships and business unions development. All these two projects are there to reward our members when we sponsor their families to meet them abroad. We cover all the cost of family sponsorship, deal with the best lawyers and help with paperwork. We know the best lawyers, so your family will be here in no time. Selection of sponsored families is based on situation and need. Some of our members who want their family selected quickly may agree to involve in the sponsorship process. We work hand in hand with you to bring your family quickly.

Another benefit our members enjoy is being a shareholder in the businesses associated with our charity. These businesses help support our charity and provide jobs to refugees. In time, we will all enjoy the profit. Business proposals we have are a taxi chain, a grass hatched sudanese hut-like hotel in the refugee camp which will give the humanitarian refugees a simillar feeling to that of the refugees. We also have a proposal of starting a school in and out of the camp. Another exciting business proposal is a Radio station in and outside the refugee camp. Its exciting to be part of refugee stars. We welcome you to be part of us. We are all a family.
